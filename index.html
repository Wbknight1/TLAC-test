ABOUT US

We are striving to bring personal fitness to your doorstep. We at TLAC want to ensure that trainees get the most out of their workouts, and that trainers have access to a flourishing market to accomodate their expertise and importance.

About TLAC

We created Train Like a Champion to connect trainers who strive to get the best out of everyone with people who want the best brought out of themselves. We felt it was important to use the connectivity of today's online environment to empower people to better themselves.

Our Team

We were created by four students at the University of Alabama in 2024: Brady Marsh, Keelan Dubs, Wheeler Knight, and Jack Libby. We saw the need to bring personal fitness and growth to everyone, and make getting fit easier than ever.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to make sure that not one person feels lost or misguided on their personal health journey, and that not one iota of knowledge goes unused. Everyone deserves to be healthy and happy, and we will do whatever we can to make that a reality.
